Big Data

Keqing Liu(Claudia)

Big data is a term that describes the large volume of data

Big data can lead to better decisions and strategic business moves

The concept gained momentum in the early 2000s

Why Is Big Data Important?

1. cost reductions

2. time reductions

3. new product development and optimized offerings

4. smart decision making

Who Uses Big Data?

1.Health Care(patient records; treatment plans; prescription information)

2.Education(at-risk students; make progress; better system)

3.Government(manage utilities; run agencies; traffic congestion; prevent crime)

3 Ways Big Data Will Transform Business

1. Big data expands customer intelligence

2.Big data improves operational efficiencies

3. Big data + mobile means new business processes

· huge amount of data that is manufactured by different devices and applications

· widely used into public institutions

· promote companies to make efficient and effective strategic decisions
